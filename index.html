HOW IT WORKS

ENSO’s non-invasive neuromodulation treatment consists of high frequency electrical pulses delivered across the skin to suppress the activity of the nerves in the painful area.

How does it feel?

This nerve suppression is experienced as a slight sensation of numbness, and is different from the mode action of traditional forms of neuromodulation treatments such as TENS devices.
